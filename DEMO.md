# 🎬 Demo Script — Student Second Brain V2

## Setup Checklist (Before Demo)
- [ ] Flask backend running (`python app.py`)
- [ ] Chrome extension loaded and pinned to toolbar
- [ ] Notion workspace open in another tab
- [ ] A YouTube lecture tab open (any CS/math lecture)
- [ ] A Codeforces problem tab open (any problem)
- [ ] A GFG or Wikipedia article tab open

---

## Demo Flow (5 minutes)

### Scene 1 — Smart Page Capture with Subject Selection (60 seconds)
- **What tab to open**: A GeeksforGeeks or Wikipedia article on Graph Traversal.
- **What text to highlight**: Highlight the first two intro paragraphs explaining the core concept.
- **What to click**: Click the Student Second Brain extension icon, select "Data Structures" from the dynamic Subject dropdown, and click "Capture to Notion".
- **What appears in Notion**: Switch to the Notion tab to show a beautifully formatted card inserted into the Data Structures database, complete with an AI-generated 3-sentence summary and 3-5 topic tags extracted directly from the highlighted text.

### Scene 2 — YouTube Lecture Capture (60 seconds)
- **What YouTube video to open**: A 45-minute MIT OpenCourseWare or similar YouTube math/CS lecture.
- **What to click**: Open the extension and just click "Capture YouTube Lecture".
- **What the AI summary looks like**: The AI successfully distills the giant transcript down to the 3 absolute core takeaways and generates specific topic-level tags.
- **What appears in Notion**: A new page in the Student Notes database titled with the actual video name, containing the clean transcript and the AI's 3-sentence summary prominently mapped at the top.

### Scene 3 — Codeforces Problem Tracker (60 seconds)
- **What CF problem to open**: Any Codeforces problem page, like "A. Watermelon".
- **How to mark it unsolved**: Open the extension on the problem page, ensure the status dropdown says "Unsolved", and click "Capture Codeforces".
- **What appears in CP Tracker**: A new structured card pops up in the "CP Tracker" Notion database.
- **What problem summary looks like**: The Notes section contains a pure, constraint-focused summary of the problem statement and the I/O format generated by the AI—with absolutely zero solution hints or algorithm spoilers.

### Scene 4 — Dynamic Subject Database Creation (45 seconds)
- **Open extension**: Stay on the Codeforces page or any blank tab, and open the popup.
- **Select dropdown**: Click the Subject dropdown and select the "+ New Subject" option.
- **Type**: Type "Quantum Physics" into the text bar that appears.
- **Click Add**: Click the "Create Subject" button.
- **Show Notion**: Switch over to Notion to show the Master Index updating live, and a brand new database appearing under the parent page with the exact correct schema.

### Scene 5 — Natural Language Search (30 seconds)
- **Go to Query tab**: Open the extension and switch to the Search/Query tab.
- **Type**: Type "graph traversal algorithms".
- **Show results**: The extension instantly returns relevant captured cards matching the query.

### Scene 6 — Exam Dashboard + Google Calendar (45 seconds)
- **Go to Dashboard tab**: Open the extension and switch to the Dashboard/Calendar tab.
- **Show upcoming exam cards**: The extension displays upcoming exams pulled dynamically from the Notion Calendar database.
- **Open Google Calendar**: Switch to your browser's Google Calendar or show your phone screen to the camera.
- **Show the exam event**: The exams are visibly synced to Google Calendar with real reminders attached.

## The Superpowers Moment

Student Second Brain V2 completely collapses the distance between discovering knowledge and structurally organizing it perfectly. What makes this so uniquely powerful is how actively it builds your workspace: the Notion MCP isn't just writing rows, it's dynamically provisioning entire subject databases on the fly based on your immediate needs. By putting Gemini 2.0 Flash directly in the middle of the capture pipeline, all raw content—whether a dense PDF, a 45-minute YouTube lecture, or a math problem—is instantly transmuted into clean, searchable, summarized intelligence entirely behind a single click.
